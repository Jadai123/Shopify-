## 3. Co-Op Escrow Policy & Payments

To protect both our international buyers and direct-from-factory merchants, all transactions on Social Shopperfy are governed by our **Co-Op Escrow Protocol**:
1. **Funds Placement:** Upon completing checkout, the buyer's funds are secured inside the Social Shopperfy Co-Op Escrow wallet or trust account.
2. **Delivery Period:** The selected vendor has a specified window to ship the product and supply valid tracking data.
3. **Ledger Dispersal:** Funds are only released to the vendor's ledger account once the buyer confirms delivery, or after the automatic escrow release period expires (typically 14 to 30 days depending on the shipping corridor).
4. **Disputes:** If an item is not received or deviates substantially from specifications, either party may invoke a dispute, which is mediated by a Co-Op admin via the Admin Panel.
