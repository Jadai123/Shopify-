## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Information You Provide to Us
* **Onboarding & Persona Preferences:** When you onboard, we collect your selected shopper profile/persona (e.g., "Budget Slash" or "Value Specs") and your swipe interaction telemetry to customize your product recommendations.
* **Transaction & Checkout Data:** To complete orders, we collect your name, shipping address, email address, phone number, and selected payment/escrow method.
* **Vendor Registrations:** If you apply as a merchant, we collect business registration documents, contact details, bank accounts, and factory/inventory source locations.

### B. Automatically Collected Information
* **Interaction Logs:** We collect data on your swipes (likes, skips, saves), items viewed, chat inputs, and negotiation patterns.
* **Device and Browser Info:** IP address, browser type, device identifiers, and basic session metrics.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

* **Personalization Engine:** We feed your swipe and category interests into our AI model (using the server-side Gemini API) to generate tailored negotiation strategies, alternative vendor offerings, and specialized discount incentives.
* **Order Fulfillment & Escrow Routing:** We share your contact and shipping information with the specific verified vendor supplying your item, and with our co-op escrow processors to verify delivery before dispersing funds.
* **AI Chat Assistant Context:** Your chat transcripts are processed server-side via the Gemini API to help answer catalog questions and facilitate micro-negotiations.
* **Administrative Analytics:** To enable platform admins to audit system performance, monitor daily transactions, and resolve customer grievances.

---

## 3. Data Sharing & International Transfers

Social Shopperfy is a globally distributed co-op. Your data may be transferred, stored, and processed across:
1. **Lagos, Nigeria (Co-Op HQ):** Core administrative ledgers and escrow tracking.
2. **United States & United Kingdom:** Cloud hosting servers and premium beauty/sound merchant services.
3. **China & UAE:** Direct-from-factory smart micro-tech and luxury material verification nodes.

By using the platform, you consent to your information being transferred to these locations. We utilize contractual safety guards to protect your personal details.

---

## 4. Co-Op Escrow & Security

All transaction data, chat logs, and merchant records are stored securely. We implement standard industry security configurations to prevent data loss or unauthorized access. However, please remember that no web transmission or cloud storage is 100% secure.

---

## 5. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your region (e.g., NDPR in Nigeria, CCPA in California, GDPR in Europe), you have the right to:
* **Access & Portability:** Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
* **Correction or Deletion:** Update inaccurate info or request complete account erasure.
* **Onboarding Reset:** Easily purge your swipe history and persona by clicking the "Onboard" button in the main header of the web application.
